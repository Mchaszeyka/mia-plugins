# Win/Loss Synthesizer — Claude Cowork Plugin

Turns raw win/loss interviews or call transcripts into a structured competitive intelligence brief — not a summary, a brief with pattern frequency, objection handlers, and ranked recommendations.

**Built by:** [Mia Chaszeyka](https://miachaszeyka.com) — Marketing Operations & AI PM

---

## What It Does

Paste in one or more win/loss transcripts, Gong/Circleback summaries, or interview notes. Get back:

- Win and loss patterns with frequency counts
- Decision criteria ranked by importance
- Competitive signals by competitor with threat level
- Top objections with recommended sales responses
- Buying committee dynamics
- Ranked action items by owner (Product / Sales / PMM)

Works on a single interview or a batch. Cross-references patterns when multiple interviews are provided.

---

## Installation

```bash
/plugin install https://github.com/miachaszeyka/mia-plugins/win-loss-synthesizer
```

---

## Usage

```
/win-loss-synthesizer:analyze [paste transcripts or notes]
```

Or use naturally in Cowork — upload your interview files and ask for a win/loss brief.
